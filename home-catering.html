Special Occasion Catering

With Sugar Love, you get to enjoy those special occassions. Leave the food to us. Our expert caterer's can deliver and unpack leaving everything ready for you to serve, or we can provide full catering service also.

From birthday parties and baby showers to company functions and lunch meetings, whatever the occasion, make it perfectly delicious with us.

We offer a wide range of finger and buffet style foods to suit all tastes and budgets. We pride ourselves in only using the best ingredients, locally sourced where possible. We also provide a wide range of homemade goodies and tasty treats, suitable for all dessert and catering options.
