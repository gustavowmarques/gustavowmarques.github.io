History

The Sugar Love bakery was founded in the late 1930s when then-President Crosgrave wanted to bring to Ireland the same European delicassies that he enjoyed in cities such as Paris and London. He made the ownership of this bakery part of his conditions for taking on the office of President and for a time this fine example of cuisine was only available to the President and his staff. Once his term in office ended, Cosgrave sold the ownership of the bakery so that the people of Ireland might enjoy the fabulous flavours that Sugar Love had to offer. Since the transfer, the Sugar Love bakery has withstood a world war, rationing, times of economic crises and still remained a pillar of success on the Emerald Isle. It served as an example of triumph during the Celtic Tiger period of the 1990's and even survived due to smart business accumen when the bubble burst in the early 21st century. The Sugar Love bakery is an indellible part of Irish culture, known now throughout the world. Though more outlets have opened throughout the country and on other shores, the flagship store on Dublin's main thoroughfare remains the shining example of fresh, robust service. The Grafton Street branch has been the home of the Sugar Love since moving there in the late 1970's and has received many distinguished guests throughout the years. On her recent Royal Visit, Queen Elizabeth II of England declared the crumpets she sampled (with a fine Irish breakfast tea) to be the best that she had ever tasted. She was quoted as saying 'One wonders how such finery could be kept so secret. Surely, had we known of Sugar Love, we would have strove to take it with us.' It is not just Queens who visit the Sugar Love. President Obama could be seen in the bakery during his trip to Ireland, declaring the bakery as a shining example of the strive to freedom in the world. The Sugar Love bakery invites you to join them as they move forward in their quest to secure their position as the best that baking can offer in the world. Pop into one of their many branches, or to their headquarters on Grafton Street, and you are sure to receive the best service, the best prices and, most importantly, the finest cuisine that the world has ever seen!
